OUR STORY

Glasgow Cuisine specializes in Scottish restaurant showcasing techniques rooted in Scotland but influenced by our regionality, carefully sourcing only the best ingredients from across the atlantic and from the UK. Building off the vibrant food scene of Glasgows Neo-bistro movement, Glasgow Cuisine displays an inspiring take on Scottish cuisine in a relaxing environment. This enables our genuine approach to food & hospitality to shine.

Hassan Khaliq is the Owner of this restaurant and only recently took over the ownership of this restaurant from his late grandfather who ran this restaurant since 1965. Ingrediants are the secret to the success of Glasgow Cuisine and the ingrediants have been inherited from his ancestors who were well known cooks of their time. Glasgow Cuisine started off as a Scottish Cuisine but with time imported recipes from around the world to suit the needs of customers who come to Glasgow Cuisine both local and from around the world from as far as Australia, France and the US.

Recently Glasgow Cuisines has won many awards including 5 star Michelin Awards 7 times. Also the restaurant has won many National awards including the Good Food awards, Restaurant and Bar design awards. The Tiffin Cup. The Food Network Awards. The Restaurant's Readers' Choice Award 5 times since 2010. Glasgow Cusine is also the most popular restaurant in Scotland with people coming from Edinburgh, Aberdeen, Dundee to enjoy the unique dining experience and unbeatable food taste. Glasgow Cuisine has further plans to expand globally to meet the demand of the customers and to reach the level that can match the quality of the restaurant.
